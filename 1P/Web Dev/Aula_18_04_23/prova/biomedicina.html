Duration of the course
A Biomedicine course lasts for eight (8) semesters, which is equivalent to four (4) years of studies. During the study period, specific practices of the professional's daily life, such as basic health care, Biosafety and Collection Techniques, will be available.
The Biomedicine course is aimed at understanding the causes, consequences, and symptoms of diseases that affect the health of the population. It is an area that becomes responsible for developing mechanisms to achieve diagnoses, as well as improving treatments.

Distinguishing Features of Biomedicine College
The Biomedicine undergraduate program has a pedagogical project that is constantly updated, which contributes to the profile of education within the expectations of the job market, attentive to the latest research trends.
The highly qualified faculty, composed of masters and doctors, provides a solid theoretical foundation and modern and well-equipped didactic laboratory facilities, in which theory and practice are perfectly integrated.
